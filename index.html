How to register?
 

1. To register for any event, click on the 'Register' button, select the category and click on the event you wish to register for.
2. A smaller box will appear, select the number of participants in your team, and hit 'Okay'.
3. You will then be redirected to 'Townscript.com', please select the 'Quantity', fill in the billing and participation info. You can then make the payment on the next page.
